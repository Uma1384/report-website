Top Achievement

SR Capital — C&V nearly doubled

SR Capital School's Comprehension & Vocabulary average grew from 11.0 (baseline) to 21.4 (endline) — nearly doubling across all grades. Grade 4 alone grew from 9 to 28. This is the single strongest assessment improvement in the CCS cohort.

Engagement Leader

Hari Vidya Bhawan — 43.6L minutes

HVB dominates both total (43.6L) and per-student (3,624 mins/student) reading engagement. With 83.9% assignment completion across 5,982 assignments, HVB sets the benchmark for programme engagement. C&V improved from 12.6 to 16.8.

Priority Action

ORF decline in upper grades — investigate

Multiple schools (HVB, SR Capital, KPS Convent) show declining ORF averages — particularly in Grades 4–8. This warrants targeted oral reading fluency intervention. Angel Public (Gr 1–5 only) is the only school showing consistent ORF growth across all phases.

2026–27 recommendations: (1) Conduct Endline assessments for KPS Convent and Oxford Angel School. (2) Investigate ORF decline patterns in upper grades across HVB, SR Capital, and KPS. (3) Increase SR Capital assignment completion from 22.4% — establish a structured daily assignment routine. (4) Leverage Suman Vidya Mandir's strong ORF foundation (Grade 3: 5.2) for new school cohort. (5) Target 1 crore+ total reading minutes in Year 2.
